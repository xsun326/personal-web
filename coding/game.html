Camer

My initial idea for Camer came from watching videos on short-video platforms. Many people film as if the camera is attached to their heads, recording various vlogs from a first-person perspectiveâ€”it's quite ironic. However, during the creative process, I realized something deeper: in this era of advanced technology, cameras make every move people make feel like it's under a magnifying glass, with nowhere to hide.

This design philosophy ultimately gave birth to Camer. When people raise their cameras to record trivial moments to compare with others, it's as if everyone is completely exposed, with no privacy left.
